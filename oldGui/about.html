    I graduated from Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo in December 2013 with a degree in Computer Science. I am passionate about security (finding and exploiting both application and network level vulnerabilities) and developing software. I also love learning new things.
    I have participated in over 10 hackathons; most recently I attended Startup Weekend Santa Barbara, my 5th Startup Weekend. At each and every hackathon, I learn something new and meet some of the most incredible people. I have been drawn to entrepreneurship from a young age because of the exciting challenges and opportunities to express my creativity.
    This is also why I enjoy security so much. The field is constantly evolving and changing, which makes finding the most recent vulnerabilities demanding, but to me that is fun and exciting. I have only recently moved my focus from client-side security to server-side application security. However, I do not think that puts me at a disadvantage because of my ability to learn quickly and desire to do so.
    I am proficient at C, Java, and Android development, but have experience in many other languages. Most recently I have been studying for my CISSP certification and learning Ruby on Rails, PHP, and JS. Check out some of the projects I have worked on.
     My greatest nontechnical skill lies in my ability to work around roadblocks. I have never given up on a project, software or hardware. I use the resources available to me to overcome any challenge, which has led me to near perfect scores on all of my programming assignments.
    When working in a group, others often describe me as a perfectionist, which I believe to be accurate as I always strive for the best possible outcome.
    I am currently looking for new employment opportunities both in security consulting and software development. If your company has an opening, feel free to reach out to me at sd [at] sunjay [dot] io.

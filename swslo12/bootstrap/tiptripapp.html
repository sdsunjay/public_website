Why Tip Trip?

Our friends are some of the most important people in our lives. As a result, their safety is of primary importance. Tip Trip enables friends to better find one another during times separation and miscommunication. Whether it is a night on the town or a rave in the woods, Tip Trip connects drivers and riders with the touch of a button.

How the App works

Tip Trip offers riders a quick and easy way to request a ride home from their friends using GPS tracking and pre-set push notifications. Drivers can communicate their area and time of operation along with any desired payment to their friends who are looking for rides. The best part is, it's all between friends. Learn more.

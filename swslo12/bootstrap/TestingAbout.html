About the Team

Our team first met in sunny San Luis Obispo during Cal Poly's first annual Startup Weekend. Living in a college town, we all witnessed the prevalence of drunk driving and experienced the frustration of trying to pick up an intoxicated friend who could not communicate their location. Our answer to the problem was to make a mobile application that was so simple even the most impaired rider could use it, and to incentivize drivers by allowing them to name a payment for their services and providing them with a convenient map of their friend's location.

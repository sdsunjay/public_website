About the Tip Trip Team


Where it all began...
In sunny San Luis Obispo, our team first met during Cal Poly's first annual StartUp Weekend SLO. Living in a college town, our team had witnessed the prevelance of drunk driving and the difficulties that many students experience while being a DD.


Why we are here...
Our goal is to simplify the process of offering and accepting sober rides with your friends (and to make it fun, too!). Download Tip Trip, share it with your crew, and give it a try!

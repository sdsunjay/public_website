SLOMO

San Luis Obispo Farmer's Market

In November 2013 I participated in Mindbody’s first ever Hackathon. There were prizes in 3 categories: best wellness app, best local app, best design. I did not have a team going in, but formed one when I got to the event, which was held at Mindbody’s campus. I worked with Edward Huang, a Graphics Communications major, Kevin Navero, a Software Engineer major, and Alles Rebel, a Computer Engineering major. It was not my first time using PHP, I had done a smaller project using it. However, it was my first time using PHP to interact with MySQL. I had to ask for help a few times. I asked things like what the difference was between POST and GET and which was more secure. I also learned how to parameterize queries in PHP. In the end, the hackathon turned out to be more of a learning experience. It was also a chance for me to become more familiar with Mindbody and the company's culture. I also met some really look people, such as John Hammerlund, who is a rockstar. Although we did not win, I am still very proud of our team and what we got accomplished. Many thanks to Edward for making it look so good. You can see our (unfinished) webapp here!

What We Do

Using advance sentiment analysis software, we provide population mood data to various industries for expansion and management purposes.

How it works

Taking each individual tweet in a geographic area, our software catagorizes it into either a positive or negative feed. The collective mood can predict economic prospects of regions similiar to the consumer confidence index.

Startup Weekend SLO Fall 2016

Startup Weekend SLO Fall 2016 was my 5th Startup Weekend SLO event. This year I did not pitch, but instead wanted to find a really interesting idea. Initially I had a hard time choosing a team. After listening to everyone's idea, I felt Elliot's idea was the best. Elliot was looking to solve the problem of students cramming for tests. He had done some investigation and found that if students were given a question each day to answer, they would remember the material better. Elliot, myself, Petar, Monty, Ross, and Jeff. Petar and I worked hard on a POC using Ruby on Rails, while the other members on our team worked on the presentation and Business model canvas. At the end of the weekend, we had a working prototype, which you can find here. Despite some technical difficulties midway through our presentation, we were able to impress the judges and place third. Our team is committed to making our web app a reality and hopes to release soon. Stay tuned!

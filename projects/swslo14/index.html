Startup Weekend SLO 2014

Startup Weekend SLO 2014 was my 3rd Startup Weekend SLO event. I like to try something new each startup weekend. This time I wanted to learn a new language. Ian Mitchel, a friend and rockstar developer, was also participating, so I wanted to team up with him. He introduced me to Ashley McCarter, who had come all the way from Denver, CO to take part. Ashley's idea was that in this growing sharing economy, people are more willing to share and trade, so it'd be great to create a website to facilitate that. People with a need would be able to easily connect to others with a skill to get a job done. For example, instead of Ashley paying a babysitter for an hour of babysitting, she could clean their house for an hour or teach them a sport or instrument for an hour. I also liked this idea, so Ian, Harry, and I worked on a Ruby on Rails application to make it happen. Voila, Karmik (formerly Orange) was born. All of our code is on Github, check it out here.

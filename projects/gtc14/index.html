GTC 2014

GPU Technology Conference 2014

GTC 2014 was my second time at GTC. I decided to go again because I enjoyed it so much the previous year. This year I tried to focus more on attending areas that were really of interest to me. I attended several talks on high-frequency trading using GPUs and cracking passwords with GPUs. None of those talks were really that memorable. What was really memorable was the keynote from Jen-Hsun Huang. He talked about how GPUs are affecting our lives on a daily basis whether we realize it or not. GPUs are now being used in Tesla s Model S Tegra 3 powers the 17-inch LCD touchscreen, at Audi Tegra X1 s are being used to interpret signals for 'self-piloting capabilities', and most impressive is the use of GPUs in Oculus Rift. The number of ways GPUs are being utilized continues to expand. While I skipped the conference in 2015 (due mainly to work ), I fully intend to attend in 2016.

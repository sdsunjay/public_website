SWSB14

Startup Weekend Santa Barbara 2014

Our team first met during the fourth annual Startup Weekend Santa Barbara. We came together to figure out an elegant solution to losing our bicycle lock keys. We have all lost our keys and it is a horrible experience. It is even worse to have to have your bicycle lock cut because of it. Our innovative solution solves that problem by using RFID technology imbedded in a ring, a card, or a sticker which will sync with your smart u-lock and open it. Find out more here.

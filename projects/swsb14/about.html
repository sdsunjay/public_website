About the Team

Our team first met in sunny Santa Barbara during the fourth annual Startup Weekend. We came together to figure out an elegant solution to losing our keys. We have all lost our keys and it is a horrible experience. It is even worse to have to have your bicycle lock cut because of it. Our innovative solution solves that problem by using RFID technology imbedded in a ring, a card, or a sticker which will sync with your smart u-lock and open it.

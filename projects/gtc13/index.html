GTC 2013

GPU Technology Conference 2013

GTC 2013 was my first time at GTC. I decided to go because at the time I was taking a parallel programming class in CUDA at Cal Poly with Dr. Lupo. The conference was really fun and I learned a lot. I was also really impressed with what people are doing with GPUs and CUDA. It was awesome seeing people push the limits of what they thought was possible. GPUs allow for massive parallelization, which can result in an exponential increase in performance. You can see even more pictures from the event here.

By using the ExecutionTime metric, an alarm is triggered if the Spark Job's execution time is outside of “normal”. The alarm sends a notification to SNS, read by Chatbot and sent to a configured Slack workspace/channel. The on-call engineer can view the message on Slack via computer or phone and begin troubleshooting steps before the job has failed.

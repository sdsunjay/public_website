About Sunjay Dhama

The Friend you haven’t met yet

Welcome. I currently work at Stanford University in the Information Security Office. I am also working on several side projects as well as taking a python course. I make a sincere effort to stay current with security news, such as the newest vulnerabilities.

I graduated from Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo in March 2014 with a degree in Computer Science. I entered Cal Poly with no programming experience, but through hardwork and determination was able to excel in programming assignments. Throughout college I competed in numerous hackathons and Startup Weekends (See some pictures from these events here). I enjoy these type of events because at each one I learn something new and meet some of the most amazing people. I have been drawn to entrepreneurships since a young age because of its unique challenges. This is also why I enjoy security so much. The field is constantly evolving, which makes it exciting.

Tracking Project

Keeping tracking of You!

This project stems from lack of analytics I have regarding vistors to my website. While I do utilize Google Analytics, in order to get more precise information such as how long a user spends on a page and what links they click on while on that page, you must pay. I thought that was ridiculous and would be pretty easy to implement using JavaScript and a bit of PHP. I am proud to say that most of the implementation is complete (I currently get and store information) and that I only need to integrate and test it. However, this often takes the most amount of time and my attention has been focused on other projects. If you want to see the small amount of information I am going to be collecting, you can go here.

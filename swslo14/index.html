Karmik

Startup Weekend SLO 2014 was my 3rd Startup Weekend SLO event. I wanted to learn a new language. Ian Mitchel is an awesome developer and I wanted to team up with him. He introduced my to Ashley McCarter. Ashley's idea was that in this growing sharing economy, people are more willing to shae, but also trade. She thought it would be easy to have people with a need connect to others with a skill to get a job done. For example, instead of Ashley paying a babysitter for an hour of babysitting her children, instead she could clean their house for an hour or teach them a sport or instrument for an hour. I also liked this idea, so Ian and I worked on a Ruby on Rails application to facilitate that. Voila, Karmik was born.

About Sunjay Dhama

The Friend you haven’t met yet

Welcome. I am currently applying to grad school with the goal of obtaining my Master's degree in Computer Science and eventually my PhD. I want to focus on Machine Learning. I am working on a Machine Learning project as well as taking a Machine Learning course. In my free time I like to read, workout at the gym, and work on this website.
I graduated from Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo in March 2014 with a degree in Computer Science. I entered Cal Poly with no programming experience, but through hardwork and determination was able to excel in programming assignments. Throughout college I competed in numerous hackathons and Startup Weekends (See some pictures from these events here). I enjoy these type of events because at each one I learn something new and meet some of the most amazing people. I have been drawn to entrepreneurships since a young age because of its unique challenges. This is also why I enjoy machine learning so much. The field is constantly evolving, which makes it exciting.
